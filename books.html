New Year new me:


Hello!
Survived another year. Quarter of a decade is already over. 2026 meaning I am soon turning 26 (easy to remember). During my winter break I haven't read that much due to a lot of mental stuff and stress. But I lived!
When I was in Sweden I bought different plays of August Strindberg such as:

Miss Julie
The Dance of Death
The Father
A Dream Play
The Ghost Sonata

I think my favourite play of them all was either the Ghost Sonata or the Dream Play.
I enjoyed the plays a lot. It's quite complex and quite surreal the topics he explores. Such as Legacy and Suffering and death. Suffering seems to be a big topic always coming up in different facettes. If it's tormenting eachother as a wife and husband or suffering in general in the humanity and the humanity should be pitied.
I think seeing an actual play of Strindberg would be an experience and I will be definitly on the look-out for any plays of Strindberg and if any are near me.
I hope you also had a nice end of the year and hopefully you will have a happy and good 2026!!!
