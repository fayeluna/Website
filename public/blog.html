16.06.2026 My different Characters or Alters

I do not have DID. I just like to categorize myself into different characters or roles of who I am and also what I have to do. I usually base all my characters around different things and activities. And usually if I don't like that activity anymore usually I can't associate myself with that character anymore. So for example I used to love streaming games on Twitch. I have said multiple times that I miss being that person that was able to do that. I can't get myself to do that anymore. I tried in the last two years to stream occasionally but it would usually just lead to high anxiety and sort of a depressive mood. I was sad that I am not able to enjoy those activities anymore. This text is for me mainly to write down my thoughts and also to make things more clear for myself. Since even for me this is quite confusing and not at all very clear. I want to crystallize these thoughts and make it more clear for myself.

My alters can change depending on my mood, what I have to do or simply how I feel. I think over the time I realized that also my gender can somewhat fluctuate with all these factors and personas. Because on some days I will feel more masculine or more feminine and I will express myself usually with what I wear. So clothing for me is quite important. I can express how I feel in my brain and show it to the outside world. I have been running into the problem where I don't want to give myself any gender. That can also happen. I think that is also the reason why I like either no pronouns or the "It" pronouns. Anyways that's about all here are all my names / characters / different persona's whatever you want to call it.

09.06.2026 Thoughts about Art and my being

I don't have any self confidence. I never was a confident person even as a child I was always the smallest and most of the time the "weird" kid. I didn't fit in at school. I wasn't allowed to go to the school yard to play with the other kids. I only knew how to play sports. I was good at most sports. I did track and field for a while. I did a little biking and most importantly I played Ice Hockey. For 13 years I put everything I had into that sport. This was my life. My passion. It was my goal to become an Ice hockey player. And when I stopped: I was nothing. I had no more talents. I wasn't talented in my IT apprentice ship. I am not talented in school or academics. Even now I am not talented anymore. I try my best to do "Art". but what does that even mean. There are always people that are more "talented". And as in talented I mean people have dedicated a much larger time and work for that craft to gain that talent. I am very average in drawing or just kind of okay. I am definitely not the worst but I just don't dedicate my time to it or to anything currently.

It's sucks to suck. For me it's the worst feeling when I am bad at something. It is very discouraging. I see my peers in my class just always be better do better things and do things just differently. Although I do realize that better isn't fair to myself. A person who does something different doesn't always mean it's better or worse. It's different. But currently in this school we are getting graded so something is better and worse. Which is quite a horrible feeling. It's like a person looking at your image or art and just denying it. Almost kind of denying your past experiences and in some way denying you. You show the images to someone else and they say that the images look good and even are cool or whatever. Now that judgement of a different person does not matter academically because you already got a bad grade. And in big part that is my biggest critique of the school I visit. The grades are bullshit. It's hard to show your art you made in any span of time to anyone. Because in some way you are standing there naked and present yourself with your art. You are fully exposed and vulnerable. The more you put in your art the more personal it becomes. The more expose yourself to others to the outside of your little bubble. This can also be something quite beautiful. Bein vulnerable can be something very beautiful. You can learn a lot about yourself.

Now what does that have to do with self confidence and in extension Dysphoria. The easiest to not show too much of yourself or show some version of yourself to others is relatively easy. You just remove yourself from the work you produce. It becomes more of a chore. You can view it as something that you have to do. You tell others around you that the work you produced is not good or that you could have done better. "In an ideal time I could have produced much better work bla bla bla". You take something you love doing, creating art or making a Photo series, and just see it as work, something that you have to do. See it as a chore and as "work" and not something that you like doing. And I think in extension this is what I've been doing in my last year to one and half year at most. Even now in my bachelor work I can't find the motivation to do anything. I dread any kind of work I have to do. I dread doing the design, working on the images and just interacting with this dumpster fire (my bachelor project). And even though I am really happy with the idea of the project I still compare myself with the others. I've seen the works of the others.

I think to end this one something. I am looking forward to the summer or to fall when all of this is over :)

25.09.2025 Yipiee System and stuff
