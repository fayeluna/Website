Hello !
Welcome. I am Luna (They / It )

This site is for expressing myself and just showing different stuff that I'm currently working on. It is kind of my virtual home and my place to just be myself without any limitations. I really like making art. One day that might be Cyanotypes or just drawing on my Ipad or I'll be shooting on an analog camera. I really like photography especially the analog photography.

The mechanical aspect of the different cameras I have. The slow and and intentional process behind each picture. That you can control every aspect of the whole process. You can decide what type of film. Then you can decide how you expose it. Do you want to push or pull this specific film. What kind of Camera / Lens do I want to use? Then how do I want to develop it? How do you scan / print them.
For each process you can decide what you want and decide the outcome. This is why I like photography so much.
