♥ Twig Zine ♥

This zine explores the different variant and forms of trees, branches, foliage or twigs. It contrasts negative and positive images side by side.
The images were created with a trusty Leica R3. It was developed in a cooperation with Minolta and is basically a Minolta XE Body with Leica Lenses.
